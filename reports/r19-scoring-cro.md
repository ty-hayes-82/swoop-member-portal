## TOP 5 CONVERSION FIXES — RANKED BY ESTIMATED LIFT

| Rank | Fix | Affected Pages | Estimated Lift |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Eliminate the duplicate hero on every page.** platform-slice-00, pricing-slice-00, about-slice-00, contact-slice-00 all re-render the exact hero on first scroll. On the contact/pricing pages this buries the form and pricing under a scroll that already happened. Delete these duplicate sections entirely. | Platform, Pricing, About, Contact | Very High — removes scroll friction on the two highest-intent pages |
| 2 | **Add a CTA after every value-delivery section that has no next step.** platform-slice-10, home-slice-02, home-slice-08, about-slice-05, pricing-slice-04 all dead-end without a button. A repeating "Book the 30-minute walkthrough" micro-CTA or inline card after each section would recapture mid-scroll intent. | All pages | High — captures visitors who convince themselves mid-page but have no button nearby |
| 3 | **Make the phone field optional on the demo form.** Currently required (or strongly implied) on both home-slice-16 and contact-slice-01. Phone is the single highest-friction field on B2B forms — making it optional typically lifts form completion 15–25%. | Home, Contact | High |
| 4 | **Add a sticky mobile CTA bar.** home-mobile-full shows a very long page with no persistent conversion anchor. A fixed bottom bar reading "Book 30-min Demo →" visible from scroll position 2 onward would address the mobile dead-end gap without restructuring any content. | Home (mobile), all pages on mobile | Medium-High — mobile is likely 30–40% of traffic for this audience segment |
| 5 | **Resolve anonymous testimonial attribution before Q2 2026 or replace with a progress signal.** Three quotes currently labelled "Name withheld through Q2 2026 pilot" erode trust on home-slice-14, about-slice-02. Replace with initials + club type + member count (e.g., "GM, 380-member private club, Southeast") and remove the "pending" qualifier. Until real names publish, a "3 founding partners enrolled — case studies in progress" trust badge carries more weight than paraphrased anonymous quotes. | Home, About | Medium — trust erosion is a soft conversion kill; fixing it protects downstream lift from all other fixes |

## SUMMARY

- **Highest-performing sections:** ROI Calculator (home-slice-11), Karen Wittman save story (platform-slice-02), Member experience narrative (platform-slice-06), Founding Partner block (home-slice-13), Contact hero.
- **Biggest structural defects:** Duplicate hero pattern across all non-home pages (4 occurrences), Platform page dead-end footer, mobile sticky CTA absent.
- **Site-wide CTA density:** Adequate on home, sparse on platform (1 CTA in 11 sections), absent-at-bottom on about and pricing.
- **Overall site CRO grade: B** — Strong narrative and proof, undermined by repeated structural dead ends and duplicate-scroll waste on the conversion-critical pages.
