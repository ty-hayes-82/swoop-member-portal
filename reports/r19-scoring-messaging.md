## Top 5 Remaining Fixes by Conversion Impact

### 1. Anonymized testimonial attribution — HIGH IMPACT
**Sections:** home-slice-14, about-slice-02  
"Name withheld through Q2 2026 pilot" undercuts every word of the three strong quotes. For a B2B SaaS sell to skeptical GMs, anonymous testimonials trigger "made up" pattern matching. Fix: upgrade to initials + club size + region ("D.M., GM — 380-member private club, Southeast"), or obtain explicit permission for first name. This is the single highest-trust gap on the site.

### 2. Capability card titles are functional, not outcome-first — HIGH IMPACT
**Sections:** home-slice-03, platform-slice-03  
"Staffing & Labor" and "Revenue & Pipeline" are software category labels. Rename to outcome-first: "Staff to demand, not templates" / "Show the board what protected revenue." The ROI numbers already in the cards ($251K annualized, 223x ROI on alert) become 40% more compelling when the card title primes the outcome, not the function.

### 3. Mobile agent OS panel is unreadable — HIGH IMPACT  
**Section:** home-mobile-full  
The dark terminal-UI agent panel collapses to an illegible block on mobile. GMs frequently access vendor sites on phones. Either replace with a simplified mobile-only card version showing one agent + one dollar outcome, or replace with a static outcome stat card ("Labor Optimizer saved $3,200 in one shift"). The comparison table also disappears on mobile — critical differentiation asset that GMs on phones never see.

### 4. Platform hero lacks pain-frame before the value claim — MEDIUM IMPACT
**Section:** platform-hero  
"Every signal. One operating view." opens with capability language, not problem language. A GM arriving from a Google search lands cold. Add a single-sentence agitator above or replace the eyebrow label ("PLATFORM") with a problem statement: "When your systems don't talk, members leave without warning." The home hero proves this formula converts — platform page should mirror it.

### 5. Pricing tier differentiator buried in body copy — MEDIUM IMPACT
**Section:** pricing-slice-03  
"Swoop drafts the callback + comp + shift" is the most compelling line in the entire pricing section and it lives in 12px body copy. Promote this to the Signals+Actions tier subtitle. Also: the top-tier CTA "Book the 30-minute walkthrough" is identical to mid-tier — differentiate the top-tier CTA to "Get white-glove onboarding" or "Talk to your success manager" to justify the 3x price step.
