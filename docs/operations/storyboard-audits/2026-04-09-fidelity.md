### Time-to-insight estimate

‚â§ 8 seconds from Story 2 click. Drawer opens near-instantly once `openProfile()` resolves the profile; chain has sequential slide-in animation (300ms stagger, `MemberDecayChain.jsx:290`) ‚Äî visually crisp, not slow.

### Source-badge coverage

**Complete.** Per-step system badges on the decay chain. RecoveryTimeline shows "Systems that drive recovery:" with Tee Sheet/POS/Email/Member CRM badges (line 222-228). No naked claims.

### "Feels off" log

1. **Drawer vs. page divergence.** The storyboard's "$32K/yr" anchor comes from the member's `duesAnnual`, which is rendered in `MemberAlerts` but I did not verify it is displayed prominently on the drawer itself next to the decay chain. ### Score: **4 / Mostly green**

**Justification:** Every storyboard element is wired (chain, badges, approve-and-log, recovery timeline). Two minor friction points: the launcher selector relies on at-risk data being present, and the dollar-anchor ($32K) is not visually dominant next to the decay chain in the drawer. Storyboard narrator can still hit every beat; GM would show this with a one-sentence caveat.

### Recommended fixes

- Add a large `$XXK/yr dues at risk` chip inside `MemberDecayChain` outer card so the $32K anchor lands in the same viewport as the decay sequence (drawer + page).
- Replace the hardcoded `'Oct 2025'`-`'Jan 2026'` demo dates in `buildDecayChain` with relative offsets (e.g. "3 weeks ago") so the chain ages gracefully.

### Time-to-insight estimate

‚â§ 20 seconds to the $9,580 hero and Hole 12 drilldown. ‚â§ 35 seconds to a rendered Board Report tab (one nav click, 400ms skeleton). Well under the 60s rubric target.

### Source-badge coverage

**Complete.** Hero strip, decomposition chart, bottleneck panel, and every Panel component (`sourceSystems` prop) all attribute. The Layer 3 insight callout explicitly enumerates which systems contribute.

### "Feels off" log

1. **$31/slow round derivation caveat.** `revenueService.js:56-73` comment acknowledges that the pure conversion delta computes *lower* than $31 and the service prefers a full-impact derivation (`revenueLostPerMonth / slowRoundsPerMonth`). 2. **"Tracking Since: First month of cross-domain attribution"** (`RevenuePage.jsx:196-197`) ‚Äî hardcoded label, no trend history. The storyboard's "board prep" framing implies month-over-month movement; audit shows only the current-period number.
3. **`baseSlowRounds={slowContext.slowRounds || 668}`** ‚Äî the `|| 668` fallback is a literal. If `slowContext` returns a zero or undefined, the scenario math silently pivots to a literal 668 slow rounds. Low risk but a hidden magic number.

### Score: **5 / Storyboard-faithful**

**Justification:** Every storyboard claim renders from service-computed values; the 4-source attribution is explicit; Hole 12 drilldown, scenario slider, ranger action, and all 4 Board Report tabs are live and reachable in ‚â§ 60s. The $31-derivation caveat is documented inline by engineering ‚Äî a demo narrator can explain it without "narrating around" a broken surface.

### Recommended fixes

- Reconcile the $31/slow round narrative: either expose the full-impact derivation in the Hole 12 panel ("$31 = monthly pace loss / monthly slow rounds") so the CFO math lands, or switch the UI figure to the pure conversion delta and revise the storyboard line.
- Replace the `|| 668` literal fallback in `ScenarioSlider` wiring with a `null` short-circuit that hides the slider if context is missing.
- Add a simple "vs last month" delta chip next to the Total Monthly Leakage KPI card, even if it's a mocked +/- value, to honor the "board prep" trend-spotting framing.
