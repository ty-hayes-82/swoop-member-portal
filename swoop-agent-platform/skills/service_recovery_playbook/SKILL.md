# Skill: Service Recovery Playbook

Comp thresholds, response scripts, escalation paths, and outcome standards
for complaint resolution at private golf and country clubs.

## Comp Authorization Thresholds

Staff may approve comps without GM sign-off up to these limits:

| Role | Comp Authority |
|---|---|
| Server | Up to $35 (comp a course or dessert) |
| Dining Room Manager | Up to $150 (comp a meal or round of drinks) |
| F&B Director | Up to $500 (comp a dining event or hosted dinner) |
| Head Pro | Up to $200 (comp a round of golf or lesson) |
| GM | Unlimited (membership credit, initiation fee waiver, etc.) |

Any comp for a member with annual dues >= $15K requires GM awareness
within 24 hours, regardless of amount.

## Escalation Path

1. Server or Pro Shop staff identifies complaint
2. Department head notified within 1 hour (F&B Director for dining, Head Pro for golf)
3. GM notified within 2 hours for dues >= $10K or repeat complainant
4. 48-hour resolution target for all complaints
5. Day 7 satisfaction check-in for high-priority complaints

## Response Language

**Use:**
- "Thank you for bringing this to our attention."
- "We appreciate your patience."
- "We want to make this right."
- "Here's what we're doing to address this."
- "I'll personally follow up with you by [specific date]."

**Never use:**
- "We apologize for the mistake" (admits fault)
- "This was our fault" (legal exposure)
- "I'm sorry for the inconvenience" (too generic, sounds scripted)
- "Our policy is..." (bureaucratic, not personal)

## Repeat Complainant Protocol

A repeat complainant is any member with 2+ complaints in 90 days.

Elevate priority by one tier. Flag as REPEAT in all internal communications.
Escalate to GM regardless of dues amount. The pattern matters more than
the individual complaint.

## Goodwill Gestures by Severity

- **Minor complaint** (service was slow, food was cold): Staff apology + comp dessert or course
- **Moderate complaint** (meal was wrong, long wait, course closure inconvenience): Comp meal + GM note
- **Serious complaint** (rude staff behavior, safety issue, significant event disruption): GM personal call + comp event or hosted dinner
- **Critical complaint** (health risk, significant property issue, breach of confidentiality): GM personal call + significant goodwill credit + full incident report

## Outcome States

- `retained`: Member remains active and satisfied after resolution
- `re_engaged`: Member had reduced activity; complaint resolution drove return
- `at_continued_risk`: Complaint resolved but member still shows disengagement signals
- `resigned`: Member resigned. Always escalate to GM for post-departure outreach.
