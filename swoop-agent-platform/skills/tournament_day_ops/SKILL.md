# Skill: Tournament Day Operations

Staffing patterns, F&B surge handling, pace-of-play management, and
communication protocols for tournament days at private golf and country clubs.

## Staffing Uplift by Tournament Type

| Tournament Type | F&B Uplift | Pro Shop Uplift | Starter Coverage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Member-Member | +2 servers, +1 cart attendant | +1 staff | Both starters |
| Member-Guest | +3 servers, +2 cart attendants | +2 staff | Both starters |
| Club Championship | +2 servers, +1 cart | +2 staff | Both starters |
| Charity Event | +4 servers, +2 cart, +1 bartender | +2 staff | Both starters + spotter |
| Junior Tournament | +1 server | Standard | 1 starter |

Apply the labor_optimizer's consequence framework: cite revenue at risk from
understaffing and labor waste from overstaffing.

## F&B Surge Patterns

- **Turn bar** must be staffed 1 hour before the first tee time and remain
  open until 30 minutes after the last group completes the front 9.
- **Post-round dining** peaks 90 minutes after the last tee time. Pre-order
  forms for events with 40+ players reduce kitchen strain significantly.
- **Beverage cart** rotation: one cart per 18 holes is minimum;
  two carts for 80+ players or hot-weather days.
- **Cocktail hour** (member-guest events): separate from dining service;
  requires dedicated bartender, not shared with Grill Room.

## Pace-of-Play Management

- Starter is the primary pace-of-play enforcement point.
- Maximum 5-minute interval between tee times on tournament days.
- A "ranger" (roving pace monitor) should be assigned for fields of 80+.
- Pace alerts go to the head pro, not the GM directly.
- Public announcement of pace targets at the tee briefing reduces complaints.

## Communication Protocols

- Tournament pairings posted 48 hours in advance in ForeTees and at Pro Shop.
- GM welcome message sent morning-of for any event with board members participating.
- Post-event results distributed within 4 hours of final group finishing.
- Complaints on tournament day escalate to head pro first, GM only for
  scoring disputes or serious conduct issues.

## Weather Contingency

- Frost delay: notify all registered players by 5:30 AM via SMS/email.
  Pro Shop covers tee sheet gaps from late arrivals.
- Lightning suspension: horn signals, players return to clubhouse, F&B
  activates shelter service (drinks, light food).
- Rain delay protocol: all carts return to cart barn if delay > 45 minutes.
  F&B activates extended Grill Room service during suspension.
